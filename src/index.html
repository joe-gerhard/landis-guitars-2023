About

Sam Landis here! I’ve been learning about and working on instruments for about 14 years now. My very first job was at a small guitar shop in Gilbert, AZ where I fell in love with playing and tweaking guitars. After the owners had to sell the little place, I went to the Roberto-Venn School of Luthiery in Phoenix where I learned all about acoustic and electric guitar construction and repair.
